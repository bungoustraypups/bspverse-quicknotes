James Lennox

  • son of Naomi [Tanizaki] and Charlotte Lennox [OC]
    • oldest child of his family; older twin of Matthew Lennox
  • boy, cis (for now)
    • he/him
  • gay
  • starts the series at 11 and ends at 18
  • born May 9th, 2003
  • Namesake: Sir James Matthew Barrie
  • Ability: Peter Pan
    • Ability Details: He can fly! Among other things, I'm not really sure what I'll end up doing in the long run for his Ability. Flight is part of it, though.
  • Affiliation: Apprentices of the ADA first and foremost, and then the ADA itself later in his life, as well as with the Gunslinger Kids too. He's a pretty dedicated detective and never really even considers any other path for his life.
  • Level at which I think about them: low, though he has potential to be a cool character for sure
  • Brief Notes:
    • The type of guy who's had the wildest imagination ever since he was old enough to properly think, James is best known among his friend group for being a storyteller, and not in the lying way; he's got a knack for recounting true stories in a way that makes them feel fantastical. He likes to describe himself as "the main character," though less in a "the world revolves around me and you're just living in it" way and more in a "I view my life as if it were a story" way. He's generally well-liked, very charismatic, and friendly; all of that combined with his charming good looks makes him pretty desirable to a lot of people, and he gets an insane amount of gifts, candies, and confession letters on Valentine's day. He gets so many, in fact, that he actually saves up in preparation for buying White Day gifts all through the year, starting the day after Valentine's. Even though almost none of his Valentines are women (as most people who spend any time with him at all are well aware that he's gay and not interested in binary women), he still insists on getting White Day gifts because most of his Valentines are also gay men/nonbinary/multigender people who won't get any gifts from women as they didn't give any women any gifts. He's a pretty nice guy, all things considered.
