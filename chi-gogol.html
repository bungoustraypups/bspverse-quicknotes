Chi Gogol

  • daughter of Sigma & Nikolai Gogol
    • seventh oldest of her family; middle triplet of Phi Gogol and Psi Gogol
  • girl, cis (for now)
    • she/her
  • queer aceflux aroflux
  • starts the series at 10 and ends at 17
  • born March 23rd, 2004
  • Namesake: the Greek letter Chi
  • Ability: Plato
    • Ability Details: So like the rest of her siblings with Greek letter names, her Ability itself is named after an ancient Greek philosopher with a school of thought behind their name that they either created or was created based off of them. Plato founded the Academic school of thought, otherwise known as Academia today. I don't know what her Ability actually does yet, but it is either very similar to Sigma's Ability, or a version of his Ability that got passed down to her.
  • Affiliation: the only real allegiance Chi holds is to the Messenger Club, where she holds a Guardian role (essentially, she guards the younger and unskilled members of the Club when they are tasked with transporting a message somewhere)
  • Level at which I think about them: low
  • Brief Notes:
    • Definitely less of a troublemaker than some of her older (and younger) siblings, though this notably doesn't mean that she doesn't have a penchant for seeking out and/or causing problems at all, just that she does it less often. Chi is definitely the brains between herself and her triplet siblings, and relies heavily on book knowledge. It is said that at least half of the books in the Gogol family's personal library belong to Chi alone, and she spends most of her solo time reading. She one day aspires to be an author, taking inspiration from the bedtime stories Nikolai would tell her and her siblings when they were small.
